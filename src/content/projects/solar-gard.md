## Overview

Solar Gard is a company that sells awnings. The main goal for the website was to
showcase projects they have done in the past. The site is kept updated with new
photos every week, giving prospects an idea of what Solar Gard is capable of and
building confidence in their craftsmanship.

## Stack

Built server-side rendered with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js)
for stronger SEO, styled with Emotion.js and Material UI.
