## Overview

Vertisol Curaçao N.V. sells indoor window blinds, roller shades, and other window
treatment products. Showcasing their products online makes it easier for
customers to see what they offer — the project focused on clean product
presentation and easy navigation, enabling customers to browse the catalog and
contact the team.

Built on the same platform approach as the Solar Gard project, the site takes a
different route when it comes to imagery. Most of the product photos are
professional shots supplied directly by the manufacturer, so rather than relying
on original photography of finished work, the focus here was on presenting
that existing material in a clean, catalog-style layout that lets the products
speak for themselves.

Since a large share of visitors browse on mobile, keeping the site lightweight
was a priority. Images are optimized and served efficiently so pages stay fast
even with a large product catalog, without sacrificing the visual quality that
a manufacturer's product photography deserves.

## Stack

This project runs on SvelteKit with TypeScript.
