## Overview

Blue Frame Digital builds high-performing digital presences for Curaçao-based businesses. Recognizing that most local companies have websites that lack strategy, they focus on conversion design—turning casual visitors into actual inquiries. Their services extend beyond standard web design to include custom AI-powered business assistants (for smart quoting and automated chat), local SEO optimization, and seamless third-party integrations (like WhatsApp, CRMs, and calendars).

With over 8 years of experience, including enterprise-level work in the Netherlands, they bring a reliable, blue-collar work ethic to helping local businesses scale.

## Stack

The site features a fast, modern, and highly interactive user experience with customizable color themes (Classic Blue, Ocean Green, Workshop Wood) and light/dark modes. It is built using **Svelte 5** (utilizing runes for highly efficient state management), styled with **Tailwind CSS** for a clean, responsive layout, and deployed globally with robust SEO optimization.
